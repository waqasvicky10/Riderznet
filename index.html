Frequently Asked Questions

What internet speeds does RiderzNet offer?

RiderzNet offers speeds from 15 Mbps to 100 Mbps across our Basic, Standard, and Premium packages. All plans include unlimited data and round-the-clock customer support.

How quickly can you install my internet connection?

Our professional installation team can typically set up your connection within 24-48 hours of order confirmation. Installation includes complete setup of modem, router, and WiFi configuration.

Are there any long-term contracts required?

No contracts needed! RiderzNet offers flexible month-to-month service. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel with just 30 days notice.

What areas in Karachi do you serve?

RiderzNet currently serves major areas of Karachi including Gulshan, DHA, Clifton, PECHS, and surrounding neighborhoods. Check our coverage map or contact us to verify service in your area.
